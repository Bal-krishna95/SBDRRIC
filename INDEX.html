Our Story / हमारी कहानी

Sant Bhikha Das Raja Ram Inter College was established in 2010 with a vision to provide quality education to the rural youth of Uruwa Vaishya village and surrounding areas in Harringtonganj Block, Ayodhya.

As a private unaided co-educational institution, we proudly offer classes from 6th to 12th standard under the Hindi medium, affiliated with the Uttar Pradesh Madhyamik Shiksha Parishad (UP Board).

हमारा विद्यालय ग्रामीण बच्चों को उच्च गुणवत्ता की शिक्षा प्रदान करने के उद्देश्य से स्थापित किया गया है। हम अपने छात्रों के सर्वांगीण विकास के प्रति प्रतिबद्ध हैं।

  • Class 6: Passed Class 5 from any recognized school

Our Story / हमारी कहानी

Sant Bhikha Das Raja Ram Inter College was established in 2010 with a vision to provide quality education to the rural youth of Uruwa Vaishya village and surrounding areas in Harringtonganj Block, Ayodhya.

As a private unaided co-educational institution, we offer classes from 6th to 12th standard under Hindi medium, affiliated with UP Board (UPMSP). हम अपने छात्रों के सर्वांगीण विकास के प्रति प्रतिबद्ध हैं।

Admissions 2025–26

प्रवेश फॉर्म — Online Application

📋 Eligibility

  • Class 6: Passed Class 5 from any recognized school
  • Class 9: Passed Class 8 with minimum 45% marks
  • Class 11 Science: 60%+ in Science/Math in Class 10
  • Class 11 Arts/Commerce: 45%+ marks in Class 10
  • Age criteria as per UP Board norms

📂 Required Documents

  • Aadhaar Card (Student & Parent)
  • Previous class Marksheet & TC
  • Birth Certificate
  • Passport Size Photographs (4 copies)
  • Caste Certificate (if applicable)
  • Income Certificate (for scholarship)

📅 Important Dates

  • Forms available from: 1 April 2025
  • Last date to apply: 30 June 2025
  • Session begins: 1 July 2025
  • Document verification: Within 7 days
